Common Garage Door Issues

Understanding the common issues can help you spot problems early. Here are the most frequent problems homeowners face:

1. Broken Springs

Garage door springs do most of the heavy lifting. When they break, the door becomes extremely heavy and difficult (or impossible) to open.

2. Worn or Broken Cables

Cables help support the weight of the door. If they fray or snap, the door can fall suddenly and cause serious injury.

3. Off-Track Doors

Sometimes the rollers come off the track, causing the door to jam or hang unevenly.

4. Damaged Panels

Dents or damage to panels affect the appearance and operation of your garage door.

5. Malfunctioning Openers

If your door isn’t responding to remote commands, the opener may be faulty.

6. Sensor Issues

Misaligned or dirty sensors can cause the door to reverse or refuse to close.

Signs You Need Garage Door Repair

  • Door makes loud or unusual noises
  • It opens slowly or unevenly
  • Remote or wall switch stops working
  • Door won’t open or close completely
  • Sagging or misaligned panels
  • Jerky or delayed response
  • Visible rust or fraying on cables or springs
